Common questions

A few things people ask first.

Which service should we start with?

That depends on whether your immediate need is orientation, deeper validation, or structured ongoing support. A short conversation is usually enough to determine the right starting point.

Can services be connected over time?

Yes. Some companies begin with a Snapshot, move into validation, and later require more continuous operating support. The sequence depends on the maturity of the opportunity.

Does ongoing support mean a guaranteed amount of output?

No. Ongoing support is structured around priorities, progress sharing, and realistic work sequencing. It is designed to help teams move effectively, not to create artificial promises.
