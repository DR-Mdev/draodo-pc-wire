What matters first

Japan entry is usually a sequencing problem before it is an execution problem.

The point is to reduce avoidable cost and improve the next decision.

Three questions worth answering first

  • Should you validate demand, partner options, or routes to market first?
  • Is a direct move right now realistic, or is a phased route better?
  • Which investments would be premature at the current stage?

What often goes wrong

  • Starting activity before agreeing on the first objective
  • Treating localisation as the strategy
  • Adding work faster than the team can actively manage it
